Description

The welcoming front porch and lg entry foyer invite you into this 13 year old Colonial w open floor plan. 9' ceilings, polished HWF, custom trim and crown moldings add to elegance of this home. Fabulous kitchen with huge island is any cooks dream. On the 2nd floor the master bedroom offers a vaulted ceiling, walk-in closet and bath with Jacuzzi tub, stall shower and double vanity. There are three additional bedrooms and a full bath. This home is completed with a 1st fl laundry room, walk-up floored attic, and unfinished basement with high ceiling. All this in a convenient location - Walk to train bus, town, schools and park!
