Description

This beautifully renovated 4 Bedroom, 2.5 Bath Home is situated on an oversized tree-lined corner lot, meticulously manicured and landscaped. Located in a prime neighborhood of Westfield, the home is convenient to shopping, schools, fabulous parks and trains just minutes away for easy commuting. Guests are welcomed into the entrance foyer, where the light-filled living room with warming fireplace and gleaming hardwood floors flow into the open dining area and updated kitchen. The kitchen is appointed with custom cabinetry, granite counters, prep and seating center island, discrete powder room and stainless steel appliances. French doors from the dining area lead to a relaxing deck overlooking the level and fenced yard ? a perfect flow for entertaining. Off the dining area double doors lead to a quiet den, and a family room off the kitchen has access to the attached garage and slider doors to a slate patio in the serene grounds. The second floor hosts the master bedroom suite with 4 closets, hardwood floors and stunning master bath with dual vanity, claw-footed soaking tub and spa shower. Three additional bedrooms with hardwood floors and updated full hall bath with laundry facilities complete the second level. More living space is available to expand in the unfinished lower level with laundry plumbing. With so many amenities and updates, this lovely home is ready to enjoy in a most sought-after community.
