Description

This wonderfully renovated storybook Colonial is set on a beautiful tree lined street in a highly desirable neighborhood of Westfield and offers easy access to NYC transportation, top Westfield schools, shopping, and restaurants.  Home offers 5 bedrooms & 4.5 baths on 4 floors of finished living space    Welcome guests into the bright and airy living room warmed by a corner fireplace with granite surround and French doors that provide entre to the family room with windows on three sides as well as a cozy den.  Entertain in the formal dining room with exquisite coffered ceilings and enchanting alcove. The gourmet kitchen with abundant cabinets, granite countertops, center island, custom backsplash tile, and stainless steel appliances will please even the most fastidious chef. A sunlit breakfast area and large mudroom overlook the scenic backyard with a deck and stone patio. Recessed lighting and hardwood floors throughout first and second levels.   The master suite is complete with one single plus two walk-in closets and a sumptuous en suite bath with a custom floor, double sink vanity, soaker tub, and walk-in shower with rain shower. Three spacious bedrooms with a hall bath that boasts custom tile and a soaker tub with marble ledge complete the second level.    The third level offers an additional bedroom lit with a skylight, an office, and a full bath with custom tile and walk-in shower. Abundant closets on the third floor provide plenty of room for storage.   The newly renovated lower level includes a finished recreation room, separate exercise room, and full bathroom with corner walk-in shower. An additional storage room and updated laundry are added conveniences. A two-car garage completes this updated residence and makes it the perfect place to call home.
